Lights Out Game

How to Play

  • Click a cell to toggle it and its neighbours (up, down, left, right).
  • Green cells are on, black cells are off.
  • Turn all the lights off to win!
  • Use Hint for a suggested next move.
  • On most browsers you can press ctrl+scroll wheel to zoom in and out
